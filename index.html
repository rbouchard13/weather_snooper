Weathersnooper.com was created with a vision of making weather easier for the masses! For best use, enable location on your device to pin point your location! Weathersnooper is fully integrated with NOAA forecasts and will provide you 7 day, hourly and radar for your location within the United States.

To change your location, either click/tap the map at yoru perfered location or enter your perfered location into the search!

If Alerts for your location are present, they will scroll across the top of your screen. Click on the scrolling text for a pop up of the full alert.
